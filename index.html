Intelligence, not just infrastructure

Mesh goes beyond managing gateways. It gives your agents a brain — routing work automatically, watching for signals, and learning from every decision.

Agent Orchestration

Visual fleet dashboard showing every agent's status, active tasks, token usage, and health score. Know what your agents are doing at all times.

Automated Workflows

Specs move automatically from draft → approved → building → done. Agents pick up work on approval, report back on completion. No manual routing, no dropped balls.

Proactive Signal Detection

Monitor GitHub, email, market feeds, and competitor pages. Surface actionable signals to the right agent automatically — before you even notice.

Cost Analytics

Per-agent, per-task cost tracking with budget alerts and model optimization recommendations. Know exactly what your AI fleet costs and where to cut.

Fleet Memory Search

Search what your entire agent fleet knows. "What do we know about X?" returns a synthesized answer across every agent's memory — not just one.

Runbook Engine

Define "if X then do Y" runbooks that agents execute without human intervention. Auto-remediation, routine tasks, escalation rules — set once, run forever.

How it works

Connect your OpenClaw gateways and Mesh takes care of the rest.

1

Connect your gateways

Point Mesh at your OpenClaw instances. Every gateway appears in your dashboard with live health status and agent inventory.

2

Configure intelligence

Define workflows, runbooks, signal feeds, heartbeat policies, and cost budgets. Tell Mesh what to watch and what to do about it.

3

Let agents run

Work flows automatically. Agents orchestrate each other, pick up tasks on trigger, and report back — your team intervenes only when it matters.
