Everything you need to manage
AI agents at scale

From workspace isolation to multi-channel messaging, Project Mesh gives you full control over your agent fleet.

Workspaces

Each workspace maps to a project or business — Personal, OpTrader, Inflow, you name it. Every workspace gets its own isolated gateway, agents, sessions, and memory. No cross-contamination, full separation.

Agent Configuration

Configure agents by purpose — dev-bot, support-bot, pricing-bot. Define personas with SOUL.md, set routing rules, and bind channels. All from the dashboard, no config files or YAML required.

Multi-Channel Messaging

Your team talks to agents where they already are — WhatsApp, Discord, Telegram, Signal, and iMessage. Route each channel to the right agent automatically. No new apps to install.

Unified Task Board

See tasks from every workspace in a single cross-workspace view. Create tasks from the portal, or let agents create them from conversations. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Team Access

Invite team members with role-based access — admin, member, or viewer. Non-technical users log in to the portal to create tickets and tasks without touching any config.

Identity Linking

One user, multiple channels. Link a person’s WhatsApp, Discord, and portal identities together so agents always know who they’re talking to, regardless of the channel.

Self-Hosted

Your data stays on your machine. Your LLM API keys, your conversations, your agents. Project Mesh connects to your infrastructure via Tailscale — nothing is exposed to the public internet.

OpenClaw Powered

Built on the open-source OpenClaw AI agent gateway. Benefit from a growing ecosystem, community contributions, and full transparency into how your agents run.
