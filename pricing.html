Frequently asked questions

Do I need to host my own OpenClaw?

Yes. Project Mesh is a control plane that connects to your self-hosted OpenClaw gateways. You run the gateway on your own machine (typically behind Tailscale), and Mesh provides the management layer on top.

What LLM providers are supported?

Any provider that OpenClaw supports. Since you bring your own API keys, you can use OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Mistral, local models, or any other provider your OpenClaw gateway is configured for.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your conversations, agent configurations, and API keys stay on your own infrastructure. Project Mesh connects to your gateway — it doesn’t store your data.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately.
